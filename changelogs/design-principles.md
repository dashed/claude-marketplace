## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-14

### Added
- Initial addition to marketplace
- Skill for guiding AI-assisted UI generation toward enterprise-grade design
- Based on [claude-design-skill](https://github.com/Dammyjay93/claude-design-skill) by Dammyjay93
- 6 design directions: Precision & Density, Warmth & Approachability, Sophistication & Trust, Boldness & Clarity, Utility & Function, Data & Analysis
- Core craft principles: 4px grid system, symmetrical padding, consistent depth strategies
- Typography hierarchy guidelines (600 weight headlines, 400-500 body, 11-32px scale)
- Critical details: Phosphor Icons, animation timing (150-250ms), color meaning, dark mode considerations
- Version metadata (v1.0.0)
- Author information (Dammyjay93)
- License information (MIT)
- Keywords: ui, design, interface, frontend, dashboard, styling, enterprise, saas

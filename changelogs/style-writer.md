## [1.0.0] - 2026-05-13

### Added
- Initial addition to marketplace
- Companion skill to style-extractor for consuming stored writing styles
- Four-phase workflow: Style Discovery, Context Loading, Guided Writing, Self-Evaluation
- Smart context loading strategy (voice card first, full guide on demand)
- Task-specific guidance for short pieces, long pieces, editing, and style comparison
- Self-evaluation against stored style rubrics with per-dimension scoring
- Reference documentation: references/evaluation-guide.md with scoring process, common pitfalls, and improvement strategies

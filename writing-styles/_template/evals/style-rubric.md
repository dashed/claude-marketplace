- **Total**: Sum of all dimensions (5-25)

- **16-20**: Good match — captures the voice with minor deviations
- **11-15**: Partial match — some elements present but inconsistent
- **6-10**: Weak match — occasional echoes of the style
- **5**: No match — completely different voice
